Escape Plan

How Escape Plan works:

Escape Plan is a working prototype intend to connect people around a distance if the user needs emergency help. Anyone can register and login to the platform to view and communicate with immediate users around 500 meters.All you need to register with your First Name with email and phone number and provide a password for login credentials. Home page includes the help mates around your location given in a map right below the contact list. Click on any contacts and their details are given along their contact email, whatsapp, phone number and sms services. you can easily access helpmates by just clicking on them. Although a panic button is given at the top of the screen to access the nearest possible users in case of emergency. feel free to be a part of us. we are here to help you.
